# SANEX

**On-Chain Accountability Infrastructure for Robinhood Chain**

---

## Vision

A financial internet where no action — human or autonomous — happens without verified authority and a record of who was accountable.

## Mission

To give every on-chain participant an independent layer that verifies actions before they settle, enforces the limits they operate within, and proves what was done and by whom.

## Core Narrative

Robinhood Chain moved millions of mainstream investors on-chain and handed them self-custody and autonomous agents at the same time. Execution arrived. Accountability did not. SANEX is the neutral layer that closes that gap — not by issuing another asset, but by owning the trust infrastructure every serious action must route through.

## Problem

Mainstream users now sign transactions they cannot evaluate, delegate execution to AI agents they cannot inspect, and hold composable assets whose risks they cannot see. A single approval can be irreversible. No independent party verifies the action, constrains the actor, or records accountability. The chain shipped autonomy before it shipped a way to trust it.

## Solution

An independent accountability layer with three functions:

- **Verification** — an independent, plain-language safety verdict on every counterparty, contract, and payload at signing time.
- **Mandate enforcement** — user-defined limits on assets, size, venue, and rate of action, enforced *outside* the executing app or agent.
- **Attribution and recourse** — a portable, verifiable record of which actor acted under which mandate, and what resulted.

## Product Positioning

Infrastructure, not an asset. SANEX sits alongside execution rather than competing with it — neutral, agent-native, and portable across applications and chains. Its credibility rests on independence from the venues and agents it oversees.

## Target Users

- **Mainstream investors** who came from a protected brokerage and are unprepared for irreversible self-custody risk.
- **Agent operators** who want to delegate to AI models without surrendering control.
- **Institutions and serious participants** who require auditable, defensible records of on-chain activity.

## Key Features

- Pre-transaction risk verification with a non-expert-readable verdict.
- Independently enforced mandates for both humans and autonomous agents.
- Portable trust profile — limits, reputation, and history that travel across venues.
- Readable, verifiable audit trail for recourse and compliance.
- Integration surface for applications and agentic platforms to offer safety natively.

## Token Utility

SANEX is infrastructure first; any token exists solely to meter and govern that infrastructure, never as a speculative instrument. Utility is confined to: paying for verification and enforcement usage, staking to secure independent verifier integrity, and governing mandate standards and threat policy. Value derives from usage of the accountability layer — not from issuance or trading.

## Competitive Advantages

- **Neutrality** — trust infrastructure is only credible when it does not belong to the party being trusted; incumbents cannot self-provide it.
- **Timing** — the intersection of mainstream users, first-party agentic trading, and tokenized real-world assets makes the need sharpest here, first.
- **Category ownership** — first mover defining On-Chain Accountability Infrastructure rather than competing inside a commoditizing venue market.
- **Portability** — trust that compounds across every integration instead of resetting at each app.

## Business Philosophy

Own the layer everyone must route through and no incumbent can credibly build. Earn revenue as infrastructure — through usage, not speculation. Serve the user's interest even when it means saying "no" to an app or an agent. Build for a decade of autonomous finance, not a cycle of hype.

## User Journey

1. **Connect** — the user links SANEX to their wallet and, optionally, their agent.
2. **Define** — they set the limits they and their agents are permitted to operate within.
3. **Act** — at every signature or agent action, SANEX verifies and enforces in real time.
4. **Trust** — safe actions proceed; unauthorized or dangerous ones are stopped before settlement.
5. **Review** — every action is attributable and auditable after the fact.
6. **Carry** — the user's trust profile follows them to the next application and chain.
